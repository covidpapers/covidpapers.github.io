Posted on Thursday, April 2, 2020 One min read

I am an American with an MS in bioinformatics who has been following the Coronavirus outbreak since January.

My goal is to post digestible summaries of academic papers on COVID-19, along with some curated expert commentary.

Long term, I plan to keep an up-to-date dashboard of the current state of knowledge about the virus itself, the disease it causes, the public health response, and treatments and diagnostics coming out of the scientific literature.
