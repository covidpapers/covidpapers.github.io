I am an American CS and Bioinformatics grad student who has been following the Coronavirus outbreak since mid January. My goal here is to post interesting papers and what others are saying about them. I also plan to keep an up-to-date dashboard of the current state of knowledge about the virus itself, the disease it causes, the public health response, and treatments and diagnostics coming out of the scientific literature.
